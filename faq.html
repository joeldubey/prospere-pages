Prospere — Self-Improvement App

Getting Started

What is Prospere?

Prospere is a self-improvement app guided by Coach Celia. It walks you through guided self-reflection exercises to clarify your values and vision, then helps you set goals and build daily habits that actually reflect what matters to you.

Is Prospere free?

Yes, Prospere is currently free to use. If we ever introduce paid features, we'll give clear notice and pricing before you're charged anything.

What platforms does it run on?

Prospere is currently in beta on both iOS and Android.

How do I join the beta?

Email joeldubey@gmail.com and we'll get you set up.

Do I have to complete the exercises in a specific order?

Yes, Inner Work is a sequential path — you can't skip ahead to modules you haven't reached yet. This keeps the work coherent: you define who you are before setting goals that reflect it. You can always revisit any module you've already completed, and your progress saves automatically.

About the Content

Who is Coach Celia?

Coach Celia is the guide behind every exercise in Prospere. She's a World Triathlon Gold, Silver & Bronze medalist and 25-time USA Triathlon (USAT) National Champion across triathlon and duathlon, and she created all of the guided content in the app.

Is Prospere a replacement for therapy or professional coaching?

No. Prospere is a self-improvement tool, not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, financial, or legal advice. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a licensed professional or emergency services in your area.

How much time does it take?

Inner Work is self-paced — there's no timer or deadline. Some people move through it in a weekend, others take it exercise by exercise over a few weeks. Take your time; it's meant to be worked through honestly, not rushed.

Privacy & Account

Is my data private?

We don't sell your data or use it for advertising, and we use no third-party analytics or ad SDKs. Your content is stored securely with Supabase, our database provider — full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes, any time. Email joeldubey@gmail.com from your account's registered email address and we'll process the deletion within 30 days.

Something's not working — who do I contact?

Visit our Support page or email joeldubey@gmail.com directly with your device type and a description of the issue.
