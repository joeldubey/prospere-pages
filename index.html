User's Guide

How Prospere fits together

Getting Started

What Prospere is and how the app is organized

Prospere is a personal growth app guided by Coach Celia. You work through structured exercises to clarify your values, vision, and energy — then set goals and log daily activities that align with how you want to live your life and who you want to be.

The four tabs

  • Home — your dashboard. Swipe through widgets for goals, activities, life pie, social circle, charge meter, win box, and vision board.
  • Inner Work — the core journey. Complete modules in order to build your foundation.
  • Goals — set and track goals across time horizons (unlocks after Inner Work).
  • Activities — log what you do each day (unlocks after Inner Work).
New here? Start on Inner Work and tap "Click Here To Start" on the first module. Coach Celia walks you through every exercise in detail.

Inner Work

The module journey, order, and Coach Celia

Inner Work is a sequential path — from gratitude and values through vision, purpose, chargers vs. drainers, life pie, and your win box. Each module builds on the last.

Why the order matters

You cannot skip ahead to modules you have not reached yet. This keeps the work coherent: you define who you are before setting goals that reflect it. You can always revisit any module you have already completed.

Parent modules

  • Values — includes My Core Values and Values & Time Audit.
  • Chargers vs Drainers — five sub-modules from people and activities through your social circle and charge meter.

Coach Celia on every screen

Tap "Click Here To Start" at the top of any module for Coach Celia's full explanation of that exercise — why it matters and how to do it. The User's Guide covers how the app works; those modals cover why each exercise matters.

Your progress is saved automatically. Back and Save & Exit both preserve your work.

Your Home Dashboard

The carousel widgets and when they populate

Home is a swipeable carousel of live widgets — a snapshot of your progress without opening each module.

What each widget shows

  • My Goals — progress toward your goal horizons.
  • My Activities — recent activity logging.
  • My Life Pie — how you allocate time across life areas.
  • My Social Circle — chargers and drainers around you.
  • My Charge Meter — your overall energy balance.
  • My Win Box — accomplishments you have logged.
  • My Vision Board — images and vision from Inner Work.

Widgets unlock and fill in as you complete the related Inner Work modules. Tap a widget to jump straight to that module or hub.

Tap your avatar or the gear icon (top right) to open Profile — theme, notifications, this guide, and more.

Goals

Time horizons, hubs, and alignment

The Goals tab opens after you finish Inner Work. From there you create goals at different time horizons — from daily and weekly up to ten-year vision.

How it connects

  • Goals can tie to your Life Pie slices so effort matches what matters.
  • The Goals widget on Home tracks your active goals at a glance.
  • Coach Celia nudges you about upcoming targets if notifications are enabled.
Start with fewer, meaningful goals. You can always add more as the habit sticks.

Daily Activities

Logging actions and energy alignment

Activities is where you log what you actually do. Over time this reveals patterns between your daily choices and your values.

Logging hubs

  • Create Activities — define the actions you will take in order for your goal to be achieved.
  • Log Activities — record what you did today.
  • Dashboards — see trends across daily, weekly, and longer horizons.

Profile & Settings

Theme, notifications, tour, and data

Open Profile from the gear icon or your avatar on Home.

Appearance

Choose Dark, Light, or Auto theme. Light mode uses a brighter mountain background — helpful outdoors or in bright light.

Notifications

  • Pick a tier from Silent to All In — how often Coach Celia checks in.
  • Set Quiet Hours so nothing arrives while you sleep.
