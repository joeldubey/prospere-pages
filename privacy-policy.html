Privacy Policy

Prospere — Self-Improvement App

This Privacy Policy describes how Joel Dubey (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Prospere mobile application (“the App”). By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address, first name, last name, and the password you choose (stored as a secure hash — we never see it in plain text). You may optionally upload a profile photo.

Content you create. The App stores the self-reflection exercises you complete, goals you set, activity names, and your activity completion records. This content is personal to you and is used only to power the App’s features.

Notification preferences. If you enable notifications, your preference settings (notification tier and quiet-hours window) are stored locally on your device using your device’s encrypted storage.

We do not collect your location, contacts, health data from device health services, financial information, or advertising identifiers. We use no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you securely.
  • To store and display the exercises, goals, and activity records you enter.
  • To deliver optional local push notifications (daily reminders, streak alerts, goal check-ins) based on your preferences. These notifications are scheduled entirely on-device and do not involve sending your data to any notification service.
  • To allow you to personalize your profile.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your content for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the features you use in the App.

3. Data Storage and Processors

Your account and content data is stored on servers operated by Supabase, Inc. (United States), a data processor we use for authentication and database services. Supabase processes your data solely on our behalf according to our instructions. You can review Supabase’s own security and compliance documentation at supabase.com/security.

Profile photos you upload are stored in Supabase Storage, which is hosted in the same infrastructure.

Notification preference settings (tier, quiet hours) are stored locally on your device using encrypted device storage and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and content data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information and content from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by applicable law.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You may update your name, email address, and profile photo at any time within the App’s profile settings. You may delete your account by contacting us at the email below; we will confirm deletion within 30 days.

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or California, you may also have rights to access, correct, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights please contact us.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” below. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
